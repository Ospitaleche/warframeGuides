Welcome to Warframe Guides!

In Brozime's lastest Build refresh series, he has brought us a build for Warframe's main and most famous frame, Excalibur!

In this build, we take adventage of Excalibur's best augment Chromatic Blade, which passively adds Status Chance to his Exalted Blade, a flat amount enough to make it proc consistently its built in statuses.

Chromatic Blade also transforms all of Exalted Blade's damage into elemental damage, building for viral Heat so we can both reduce enemy armor and boost all damage when they proc.

Not only we take adventage of Chromatic Blade, but we also are running Gloom instead of his third ability Radal Javelin since Gloom is by itself a great boost to Excalibur's ability to survive high amounts of enemies by slowing everything in an area around him all the time it is active.

Not only that but it also gives Excal some lifesteal to keep him alive while also running Arcane Energize to keep his energy stock at max all time, so he can keep up both Gloom and Exalted Blade's energy costs
