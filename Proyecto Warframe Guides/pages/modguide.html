Welcome to the Modding Guide!

To mod a Warframe or a Weapon first we have to go to the Modding Screen, for that, go to your Aresenal and then hover over the piece of equipement you want to mod and click "Upgrade

Now, you should see a screen like the first picture on the right, thats your Modding Screen.

You can see there are slots on the middle of the screen, there is where the equiped mods go. you can also see you have all your avaliable mods on the bottom.

To equip a mod, just click a drag it to the slot you want to equip it on, remember that every mod has a Cost (in its left corner) and an icon on them.

Every time you equip a mod, the cost is deducted from your avaliable Capacity (Seen up left on the corner of the Modding Screen), keep in mind that equiping mods on a slot with the same icon halves their cost! but missmatching icons will cause it to cost more.
