Welcome to Warframe Guides!

To mod a Warframe or a Weapon first we have to go to the Modding Screen, for that, go to your Arsenal and then hover over the piece of equipment you want to mod and click "Upgrade".

Now, you should see a screen like the first picture on the right, that's your Modding Screen.

You can see there are slots in the middle of the screen where the equipped mods go. You can also see all your available mods at the bottom.

To equip a mod, just click and drag it to the slot you want to equip it in. Remember that every mod has a Cost (in the top left corner) and an icon on them.

Every time you equip a mod, the cost is deducted from your available Capacity (seen in the top left corner of the Modding Screen). Keep in mind that equipping mods in a slot with the same icon halves their cost! However, mismatching icons will cause it to cost more.
